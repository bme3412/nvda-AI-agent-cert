In 2023, my co-author Heather Dawe and I released “Responsible AI in the Enterprise,” which aimed to provide practical AI risk management strategies for explainable, auditable, and safe models. The landscape of Artificial Intelligence, particularly concerning its responsible development and deployment in enterprise settings, was already dynamic. However, the period from mid-2023 to May 2025 has witnessed an evolution of unprecedented velocity and scale.
